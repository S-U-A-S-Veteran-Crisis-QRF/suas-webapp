### Noted but weak fit or excluded (so the team doesn't waste time)
- **American Foundation for Suicide Prevention (AFSP) grants** — **research-only** (universities/investigators; e.g., up to $125k–$500k research awards). Not designed for a frontline service org. https://afsp.org/research-grant-information/
- **Semper Fi & America's Fund** — gives directly to **individual** wounded/ill/injured service members; does **not** make grants to organizations.
- **Veterans United Foundation** — its public program is an **individual scholarship**, not an organizational grant.
- **USAA Foundation, Boeing, Lockheed Martin, Northrop Grumman corporate giving** — veteran-relevant but **invitation-only / no unsolicited applications**. Pursue only via a warm introduction.
- **VFW Foundation grants** and **American Legion Auxiliary / ALA Foundation Veteran Projects Fund** — restricted to VFW or American Legion Family affiliates; a non-affiliated nonprofit like SUAS cannot apply.
- **The Independence Fund, Fisher House Foundation, Boot Campaign, Stop Soldier Suicide** — primarily **direct-service providers / coalition members**, not grantmakers that fund outside nonprofits. (Potential program *partners*, not funders.)
- **The Cigna Group Foundation — Improving Veteran Mental Health Grant** — real, recurring annual program on veteran mental health, but the most recent cycle has closed; queue for the next round: https://www.thecignagroup.com/our-impact/esg/healthy-society/community/foundation/improving-veterans-mental-health

## How to use this / caveats

1. **Confirm every deadline and amount on the funder's own site before applying.** Grant cycles change frequently; this report reflects what funder pages showed via web search on 2026-06-30, and most pages could not be opened directly this session (egress policy). Treat all "unverified" flags seriously.
2. **Verify SUAS's eligibility first.** Several top prospects gate on 501(c)(3) status, 2+ years of IRS 990 filings, audited/certified financials, a minimum budget, or a specific geography (e.g., Smith Trust = Western U.S. only; WWP = post-9/11 focus; Home Depot = housing + 5-year IRS standing). Confirm SUAS's tax status, state, budget, and program scope before investing time.
3. **Sequence your effort:** (a) lock down SUAS's 501(c)(3) docs and complete **TechSoup** validation — that unlocks **Google Ad Grants** and **Microsoft for Nonprofits** immediately at no cost; (b) submit the **rolling** LOIs (Bob Woodruff, PenFed, DAV) now; (c) calendar the **annual** cycles (SSG Fox next NOFO ~spring 2027; Newman's Own ~fall; APA MOORE Aug–Sep) so you don't miss windows; (d) pursue invitation-only corporate funders only via warm introductions.
4. **The SSG Fox SPGP is the single best mission fit** but its FY27 window closed June 12, 2026 — the action now is to prepare documentation for the next cycle, not to apply today.
5. **Excluded items were excluded for a reason** (research-only, individuals-only, or invitation-only) — don't spend cycles on them without a specific reason.
6. **Drafts are drafts.** Do not send the outreach emails until SUAS's facts are inserted and a person has reviewed them. This report involved no contact with any funder.
